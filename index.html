Child Support Matters

California uses a guideline formula to calculate child support, but disputes often arise regarding income, employment changes, or time-sharing percentages. Whether you are seeking support, requesting modification, or defending against inaccurate claims, experienced legal guidance is essential.

Irwin & Irwin LLP carefully evaluates financial documents to ensure support calculations are fair and consistent with state law.

Spousal Support (Alimony)

Spousal support may be awarded temporarily during divorce proceedings or long-term after final judgment. Courts consider numerous factors including the length of marriage, earning capacity, standard of living, and health of both parties.

A knowledgeable Fullerton Family Law Firm ensures that support arrangements are equitable and sustainable, whether you are requesting or contesting payments.

Property and Asset Division

Property division may involve real estate, retirement accounts, business interests, investments, and personal property. Complex financial portfolios require strategic evaluation to prevent hidden assets or improper valuations.

Irwin & Irwin LLP collaborates with financial professionals when necessary to ensure accurate asset valuation and fair distribution.

Domestic Violence and Protective Orders

Family law cases sometimes involve allegations of domestic violence. Protective orders can significantly affect custody and visitation rights. Prompt legal action is essential in these situations.

The firm represents clients seeking restraining orders as well as those defending against false accusations, ensuring due process and protection of legal rights.

Modifications and Enforcement

Life circumstances change. Income adjustments, relocation, remarriage, or evolving child needs may require updates to court orders. A Fullerton Family Law Firm can assist with modifying custody, support, or visitation agreements and enforcing compliance when orders are violated.
